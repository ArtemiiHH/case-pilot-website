# CasePilot

**Automate case updates for your law firm.**

CasePilot is a case management tool built for law firms that want to stop answering "what's the status of my case?" — and start spending that time on actual legal work.

→ **Product:** [app.getcasepilot.co](https://app.getcasepilot.co)  
→ **Website:** [getcasepilot.co](https://getcasepilot.co)

---

## What it does

When a case moves forward, your client knows about it — automatically. No calls, no emails, no chasing.

CasePilot gives every client a personal tracking link. When you update a case stage from the dashboard, they see it instantly on their end: where the case stands, what stage it's at, and the full history of updates.

## Case stages

Cases move through a standard pipeline:

`Case Opened` → `Documents Requested` → `Documents Received` → `Under Review` → `Awaiting Court Date / Filing` → `In Progress` → `Awaiting Decision / Outcome` → `Resolved / Closed`

## Who it's for

- **Solo practitioners** — Create a case once, and your client sees every update without you lifting the phone.
- **Small law firms** — Keep every paralegal and partner aligned on case status, without another status meeting.
- **Medium law firms** — Standardize client communication across every practice group. Every client gets the same professional experience, no matter which attorney handles their case.
